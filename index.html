      FAQ

      Frequently Asked Questions

      Is root canal treatment painful? +
      No — root canal at Al-Fazal is completely pain-free. We use advanced local anesthesia. Most patients are surprised at how comfortable it is.
      How long does teeth whitening last? +
      Professional whitening lasts 1–3 years. We recommend touch-up sessions every 6–12 months for best long-term results.
      What is the cost of dental implants? +
      Implants start from Rs. 35,000 per tooth. We offer flexible installment payment options to make treatment accessible.
      What age is best for braces? +
      Braces work best between ages 10–14 but are equally effective for adults. We offer clear aligners as a discreet adult option.
      Do you treat dental emergencies? +
      Yes! Open daily till 11:30 PM for all emergencies. Call 0321-4105096 any time.
      How do I book an appointment? +
      Call 0321-4105096, WhatsApp us, or click Book Now. Walk-ins welcome. First consultation is FREE!
